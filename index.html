Android Auto: Safer and Smarter Driving in Houston

Driving in Houston can be stressful, with congested freeways and distracted drivers everywhere. Android Auto makes it safer and smarter.
Safer With Android Auto, you can use voice commands to control music, navigation and messaging so you can keep your hands on the wheel and your eyes on the road.
Smarter navigation Android Auto provides live traffic data to suggest the fastest route to your destination. It will even alert you to traffic delays and suggest alternative routes to save you time.
Stay connected While driving, incoming calls and texts are announced aloud so you can choose to answer or ignore them verbally.

Hands-Free Calling and Messaging While Driving

With Android Auto, you can stay connected on the go without distraction. How? Simple - it provides hands-free calling and messaging so you can keep your hands on the wheel and eyes on the road.
Once you've paired your Android phone to your vehicle's display, you're all set. When a call or text comes in, Android Auto will announce it and ask if you want to respond. Just say "yes" or tap the touchscreen to activate the voice assistant.
